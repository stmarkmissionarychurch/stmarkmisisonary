A Rich History

Saint Mark Missionary Baptist Church has served the Vienna, Georgia community through faith, worship, fellowship, and outreach for generations.

Our mission is to honor God, strengthen families, and impact our community while living out our vision: "Reaching the Lost, Making Disciples, and Transforming Lives."

Pastor James Jordan

Pastor James Jordan leads Saint Mark Missionary Baptist Church with a heart for service, discipleship, and community outreach.

A native of Vienna, Georgia, Pastor Jordan attended Vienna High and Industrial School, Fort Valley State College, and proudly served in the United States Army, achieving the rank of Sergeant.

He received his License in Christian Ministry in 2006 and his Ordination Certificate in 2010.

First Lady Ava Felicia Jordan

First Lady Ava Felicia Jordan is committed to ministry, outreach, biblical teaching, and encouraging others through faith and service.

She earned her undergraduate degree from Talladega College and later completed a Master's degree in Organizational Psychology from Capella University.

Together, Pastor and First Lady Jordan continue to serve Saint Mark with love, compassion, and dedication.
